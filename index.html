How do I register my food business with NAFDAC?
AI
To register a food product with NAFDAC, here's what you need:

1. Pre-registration — Register your company on the NAFDAC e-portal at nafdacportal.gov.ng.
2. Product dossier — Prepare a technical dossier including product composition, manufacturing process, and lab test results.
3. Factory inspection — NAFDAC will inspect your production facility before approval.
4. Fees — Registration fees range from ₦50,000 to ₦500,000 depending on product category.
5. Timeline — Expect 3–6 months for full registration.

Want me to walk you through the dossier requirements in detail?
U
Yes please, and what documents do I need?
AI
For the NAFDAC dossier, you'll need: ✓ CAC certificate of incorporation, ✓ Product label (must comply with NAFDAC labelling requirements), ✓ Certificate of Analysis from an accredited lab, ✓ Manufacturing SOP, ✓ HACCP plan (for food businesses), and ✓ Proof of premises ownership or tenancy.
